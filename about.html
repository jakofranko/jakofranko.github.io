Our tools are built using our own modular React framework, which has been open-sourced In the past, I have worked to build and design web-sites for a number of clients as a freelance developer, and also worked on a team that built and maintained an enterprise-level internal tools suite on a LAMP application that used a custom MVC framework of our own design.
